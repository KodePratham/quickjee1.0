Capacitor or Condenser.

It is an electrical device used for storing electric charges. It consist of two conductors seperated by air or dielectric material.

Capacitance C =

$$ C = {Q \over V} $$

Capacitors in series

$$ {1\over C_s} = {1\over C_1} + {1\over C_2} + {1\over C_3}$$

Capacitors in Parallel

$$ C_p = C_1 + C_2 + C_3 $$

Capacitance of parallel plate capacitor

$$ C = {A \epsilon_0 \over d} $$

Capacitance of a parallel plate capacitor with a dielectric slab between plates

$$C = {A \epsilon_0 \over(d-t + {t\over k})} $$

Displacement current $$ I_d $$

$$ I_d = Ak\epsilon_0 {dE\over dt} $$

Enery stored in a Capacitor

$$ U = {1\over 2} CV^2 $$
