Question 1

What is the capital of France?

Answer 1

Paris is the capital of France. It is known for its art, fashion, and culture. The city is also home to iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre Museum.

Question 2

What is the process of photosynthesis?

Answer 2

Photosynthesis is the process by which green plants and some other organisms use sunlight to synthesize nutrients from carbon dioxide and water. It is essential for producing oxygen and sustaining life on Earth.

Answer 3

'Romeo and Juliet' is a famous tragedy written by William Shakespeare. The play explores themes of love, conflict, and fate, and remains one of Shakespeare's most popular works.
