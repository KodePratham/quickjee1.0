Magnetic Force acting on a charged particle moving with velocity (v)

$$ F_m = qvB sin\theta $$

Cyclotron Formula

$$ p = qBR $$ $$ mv = qBR$$

Biot-Savart Law

$$ dB = {\mu_0\over 4\pi} {Idlsin\theta\over r^2} $$

Magnetic induction due to straight current carrying conductor

$$ B = {\mu_0 I\over 2\pi R} $$

Magnetic field produced by a current in a circular arc of wire

$$ B = {\mu_0 I\over 4\pi r} \phi $$

Magnetic field of a solenoid

$$ B = \mu_0 nI $$ $$ n = {N\over L} $$
